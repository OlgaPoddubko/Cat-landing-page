About game

The browser game "The Cat that Walks by Himself" was inspired by the eponymous tale by Rudyard Kipling.
Those who are interested in its plot may read this masterpiece here.
The developers wish you to have fun while playing this game! ^-^

Rules

The Cat should eat chicken legs to gain points
The Cat has to jump over inanimate obstacles to move forward
The Cat must avoid dogs, goats, bees to save his life
The game is over if the Cat has lost 9 lives or has fallen into the water

Controls

Controls are very simple: arrow left to go left, arrow right to go right, arrow up or space to jump, mouse click to play, pause or restart.
